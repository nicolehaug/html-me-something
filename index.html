My Amazing Mexican Adventure

  • In November of 17 I hopped a plane for Puerto Vallarta with my girlfriend. Our plan was to visit for day of the dead festival and maybe do some surfing in Sayulita.

    Puerto Vallarta has art lined up along their beach in November in prep for their Day of the dead parade usually held on November 2.

    My girlfriend got her face painted, as did many locals for the festivities.

    We got to see some cool architecture. Pictured below is the local church in Puerto Vallarta.

    Our boutique hotel was a good mix of hostel meets hotel, with a large honor bar where you can make your own drinks.

    The actual day of the dead parade is not a large one, but fun nonetheless.

    My favorite part of this trip was boogie boarding in Sayulita. A very quaint, hippie ish town known for its gypsy like atmosphere and friendly locals.
